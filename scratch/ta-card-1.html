Absolute comfort
My review can only be excellent as this hotel met all our expectations. A geographical position overlooking the Nile , a reception hall and elegant bouquets that set the tone and sense of security felt as soon as the front door is passed. The welcome was very charming and very professional. The staff in all departments is of great discretion while being very efficient. We were lucky enough to be upgraded and our room had become a haven when we returned from our visiting days.
Everything is well thought out and available, whether it be the spa , the pool or the restaurants, always the same charming welcome and this great professionalism.
The rooms are extremely comfortable , spacious , elegant, with a large and functional bathroom. Cleanliness reigns everywhere and the absolutely complete and tasty breakfasts let you glimpse what the restaurant will be like.
We dined several times at the Italian restaurant, and it was delicious.
A special mention to the front desk supervisor who greatly contributed to making our stay most enjoyable.
A hotel to recommend
